Responsive HTML Email Templates

About this Project

Responsive email templates are notoriously difficult. In addition to needing to build them using only tables and inline CSS, you must also account for how various email clients and viewing devices will affect your markup.

This is a sample of one of the email templates that I created from a wireframe that rendered correctly on all major email clients and viewing devices.

Project Goals

To create a custom, responsive email template that could be imported into Hubspot, which the marketing team could then edit the content based on that week's newsletter

Featured Skills

  • Responsive table design
  • Custom Hubspot codes (for user edititng abilities)
