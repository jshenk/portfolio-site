Responsive Page with Media Queries and JavaScript

This page demonstrates my ability to make a responsive page through the use of media queries. In keeping with the "Mobile First" school of thought, this was designed to work first on mobile, then adjust once the screen gets larger than 768px.

The navigation bar also features JavaScript that puts a border around the active page.

Responsive HTML Email Templates

Responsive email templates are notoriously difficult. In addition to needing to build them using only tables and inline CSS, you must also account for how various email clients and viewing devices will affect your markup.

This is a sample of the email templates that I created from a wireframe for a marketing campaign that rendered correctly on all major email clients and viewing devices.
